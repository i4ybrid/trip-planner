---

## Presentation Summary (PowerPoint Version)

### **The Problem: The "Group Chat Graveyard"**
Most group trips die in the planning stage due to fragmented communication, indecision on activities, and the awkward friction of collecting money.

### **Our Solution: The All-in-One Collaboration Hub**
TripPlanner is a centralized ecosystem designed for the entire trip lifecycle—from the first "feeler" to the final settlement.

### **Key Strengths & Differentiators**

*   **Democratic Planning & Voting:**
    Move from "Idea" to "Planning" by letting members propose activities and accommodations. Our integrated voting system ensures everyone has a voice, building consensus before the first deposit is even paid.
*   **The Accountability Engine (Milestones):**
    No more chasing friends for answers. The app automatically schedules tiered milestones—Commitment Requests, Payment Deadlines, and Settlement Dates—based on your trip's timeline. It sends the reminders so you don't have to be the "bad guy."
*   **Integrated Financial Layer:**
    Go beyond simple bill splitting. Support for Equal, Share-based, and Percentage splits allows for complex group dynamics. With built-in debt simplification and support for Venmo, Zelle, and PayPal, "settling up" happens in clicks, not spreadsheets.
*   **Unified Group Context:**
    Stop switching apps. Real-time chat with @mentions, a shared photo/video "Memories" vault, and a unified timeline of past events and future deadlines keep everyone on the same page, literally.
*   **Flexible Governance:**
    Choose between "Open" trips for casual friend groups or "Managed" trips where organizers have full control over invites and approvals.

### **The Value Proposition**
**TripPlanner doesn't just help you plan a trip; it ensures the trip actually happens.** It reduces administrative overhead for organizers and provides transparency for members, resulting in less stress and more travel.
